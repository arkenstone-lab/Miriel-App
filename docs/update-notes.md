# Miriel - Update Notes

Community-facing release notes. Written for users, not developers.

---

## v1.0 - Launch (2026-02-19)

Miriel is an AI-powered journal that turns your daily notes into structured reflections with evidence links. Record in 3 minutes, review in 10.

### AI Journaling
- **Conversational check-in**: Miriel guides you through a 3-phase flow - Plan your day, capture Details, then Reflect on what happened
- **Auto-tagging**: Projects, people, and issues are automatically extracted from your entries
- **Smart summaries**: Daily summaries are generated on every save, with each sentence linked back to the original entry
- **Weekly & Monthly reviews**: Get a high-level retrospective of your week or month, with evidence links to the entries that matter

### Retention & Gamification
- **Streaks**: Track your consecutive journaling days
- **Levels & XP**: Earn experience points for journaling, completing todos, and generating reviews
- **Badges**: Unlock achievements as you build your journaling habit
- **Dashboard**: See your stats, streaks, and recent activity at a glance

### Todo Management
- **Auto-extracted todos**: Miriel identifies action items from your entries automatically
- **Source tracking**: Every todo links back to the entry it came from
- **Quick completion**: Mark todos as done right from the list

### Multi-language Support
- Korean and English, auto-detected from your system locale
- Switch languages anytime from Settings

### Dark Mode
- Full dark mode support across every screen
- Follows your system preference or set it manually

### Security & Privacy
- Email verification with 6-digit codes
- Login via username or email
- Password reset and account recovery
- Data export (JSON) and full account deletion
- Invite-only beta access

### Platform
- Web-first at [miriel.app](https://miriel.app)
- Responsive design - works on desktop and mobile browsers
- Native mobile apps coming soon (iOS & Android)
